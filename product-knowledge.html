Terpene Profiles — Clinical Reference
TerpenePotential EffectsResearch HighlightsClinical Nuance
MyrceneSedative, muscle relaxant, analgesicPotentiates GABA-A receptor responses; acts synergistically with THC to cross the blood-brain barrier.Primary driver of pronounced sedation. Essential for nighttime / insomnia formulations.
LinaloolAnxiolytic, sedative, anticonvulsantModulates GABAergic transmission and inhibits glutamate binding; reduces CNS excitability.Excellent for severe panic disorders, sleep onset latency, and adjunctive seizure management.
Beta-CaryophylleneAnti-inflammatory, analgesic, neuroprotectiveDirect selective CB2 receptor agonist. Reduces pro-inflammatory cytokines.Highly clinically relevant — targets peripheral pain and inflammatory bowel without psychoactive load.
LimoneneAnxiolytic, mood elevation, gastro-protectiveModulates serotonin (5-HT1A) and dopamine neurotransmission; synergistic anxiolytic action with CBD.Ideal for daytime formulations targeting anxiety, PTSD, or treatment-resistant depression.
Alpha-PineneBronchodilation, alertness, memory supportFunctions as an AChE inhibitor; preserves acetylcholine to offset THC-induced memory deficits.Prescribed to mitigate cognitive fog of high-THC doses; mild clinical bronchodilator.
HumuleneAnti-inflammatory, analgesic, anorecticInhibits COX-2 and iNOS; shares structural synergy with caryophyllene.Co-occurs with caryophyllene to amplify anti-inflammatory efficacy.
NerolidolSedative, antifungal, skin-permeation enhancerExhibits systemic sedative qualities in preclinical models.Valued in transdermal patches and topical formulations for permeability enhancement.
OcimeneAntiviral, antifungal, decongestantDeploys defensive, oxidative-stress-inhibiting properties.Associated with uplifting sativa-type profiles. Trace constituent — limited human clinical data.
BisabololAnti-inflammatory, antioxidant, gastro-protectiveSuppresses pro-inflammatory cytokines (TNF-alpha, IL-6).Excellent for dermatological topicals, oral mucositis, and gut-inflammation targeting.
